Scratch game

Intro

We had to create a game in scratch to get the understanding of the website scratch. I decided to make a shooter game where you have to survive as long as possible.

Final work

I created a game where you can move around using the WASD keys. You can swap your weapon by clicking either 1 or 2. You can shoot enemies by clicking on the right click button. You can rotate your character by rotating your mouse.
