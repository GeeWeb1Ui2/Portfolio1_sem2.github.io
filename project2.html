Wireframe for helping a movie character

Intro

We were told to think of a character from a movie that wants to solve a problem. We then had to think of a way to solve his problem using a interface. I choose Nick Fury from Avengers. He wants to build a superhero team. He wants get fast replies from heroes.

Brief on interface design

My website helps people build superhero teams. You can search through a list of popular superheros, all heros or post a job and the applicants. If you want to know their powers you can see their list of powers and skills or watch a demo video which demonstrates their powers. After you have made your choice you can then assemble the team and fight villains

Target Audience

This interface is generally aimed at superheros and/or people who want to build superhero teams. It is in place to assemble world class alliances to defeat enemies. This is a public interface and anyone can log in and view the content available.
