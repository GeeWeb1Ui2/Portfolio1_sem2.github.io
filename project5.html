Gates of BLR Fort- A Walkthrough

Intro

We visited BLR Fort and had a team project. We had to collect information, pictures and other content. We then had to make different contents from what we collected to be presented at the college for exhibition.

Brief on work done

I was doing a documentation presentation for the first week. After the first few days i got bored with that because it was too simple and i wasnt doing much work. So i started doing some illustrations. When i showed the faculty she suggested doing 3 illustration of the fort from different views and naming it "Gates of BLR Fort- A Walkthrough".

Final work

I created a documentation presentation and 3 illustrations of the fort. My friends created many other content like a short film, a 3d model, a painting, a comic book, illustrations, etc.
