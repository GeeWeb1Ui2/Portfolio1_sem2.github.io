Interactive fiction game

Intro

We were shown examples of interactive fiction games. In these games you can select different choices. Based on the choices you take the stories that come up change.

Plot picked

I picked a short story by O. Henry called "The gift of the Magi". It is a Christmas story about a poor couple who want to buy each other Christmas gifts for each other. They end up selling their most prized possesions to buy each other gifts.
